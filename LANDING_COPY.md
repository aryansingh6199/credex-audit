## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial period. We show you your savings first, ask for your email after.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine traces back to an official vendor pricing page, verified weekly. We cite our sources in the report.

**Q: Do you store my spending data?**
A: We store your audit results so you can share the URL. We never sell your data or share it with third parties. Identifying details are stripped from public audit URLs.

**Q: Why is Credex offering this for free?**
A: This tool helps startups find overspend. For teams saving $500+/month, Credex can help capture even more savings through discounted AI credits sourced from companies that overforecast. The audit is our way of showing value before asking for anything.

**Q: What if the tool says I'm already spending optimally?**
A: We'll tell you honestly. We don't manufacture fake savings. If your stack is well-matched to your team size and use case, we say so — and offer to notify you when new optimization opportunities apply to your stack.
