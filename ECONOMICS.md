# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assumptions:
- Average credit purchase: $500 (conservative — teams overspending $500+/mo will buy at least one month of credits)
- Credex margin on credits: 15-20% (sourced at discount, sold below retail)
- Gross profit per transaction: $75-100
- Repeat purchase rate: 40% of customers buy again within 90 days
- LTV over 12 months: ~$200 per converted customer (3 purchases average)

**A converted lead is worth ~$200 LTV to Credex.**

| Channel | Effort | Estimated CAC |

All Week 1 channels are $0 CAC. This tool IS the marketing — it generates leads by delivering value first.

## Conversion Funnel
Landing page visits:        1000
Audit completions:           400  (40% completion rate)
Email captures:              120  (30% of completions)
Consultation bookings:        12  (10% of email captures)
Credit purchases:              4  (33% of consultations)

At $200 LTV per customer:
- 4 purchases × $200 = **$800 gross profit from 1000 visitors**
- CAC = $0 (organic channels)
- **Payback period: immediate**

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = ~$83k/month revenue
At 15% margin on credits: need $550k/month in credit sales
At $500 average purchase: need 1,100 purchases/month
At 33% close rate from consultations: need 3,300 consultations/month
At 10% consultation booking rate: need 33,000 email captures/month
At 30% email capture rate: need 110,000 audit completions/month

**That's 110k audits/month — roughly 3,700/day.**

To get there:
1. The tool goes viral on HN/Twitter (realistic — $3k savings screenshots are shareable)
2. Credex builds an affiliate/referral program (each user who shares gets a perk)
3. Credex partners with 2-3 startup accelerators to offer the audit to their cohorts
4. SEO compounds over 12 months on "AI spend audit", "cursor vs copilot cost" etc.

## Rough Math Summary

- Month 1: 500 audits, 2 purchases, $400 gross profit
- Month 6: 10,000 audits/month, 40 purchases, $8,000 gross profit
- Month 12: 50,000 audits/month, 200 purchases, $40,000 gross profit
- Month 18: 110,000 audits/month, 440 purchases, $88,000 gross profit → ~$1M ARR run rate

The inputs are rough but the model is sound. The tool has near-zero marginal cost per audit and zero CAC on organic channels.
